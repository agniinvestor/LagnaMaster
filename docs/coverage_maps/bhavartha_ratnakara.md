## Coverage Map

### Structure per Lagna
Each of the 12 chapters encodes:
- **Planetary house placements** (~40 rules): For each of 7 planets (Sun–Saturn), key house positions and their effects specific to this lagna's functional classification of that planet.
- **Planetary yogas** (~15 rules): Multi-planet combinations producing specific results for this lagna.
- **Exception/override rules** (~10 rules): Conditions that modify standard predictions.

**Minimum per lagna: ~65 rules**
